Automated Microglial Counter

Description:

This project identifies and computes statistics on specific microglial densities around the hippocampal region of the brain. I developed this tool from scratch in Python to automate the cell-counting process and image analysis. The areas of interest are stained green by a specific protein, allowing me to pull the green channel and threshold it. From there minimum sizes are filtered out and we are left with the image for analysis.

Challenges:

Before the development of this tool I had never used Python before. I had to quickly learn and apply those new skills immediately. The library I was originally trying to work with was not easily compatible with Mac so I had to pivot a different direction. In addition, I got experience working directly with who this tool would benefit so incorporating feedback and understanding their vision was an added component.

About Me!

Education


I am a new graduate from Quinnipiac University. I graduated Magna Cum Laude in May 2024 with two degrees: a Bachelors of Science in Behavioral Neuroscience and Bachelors of Arts in Computer Science.

Why Computer Science?


My Origins

After my first year of college in psychology and natural science courses similar to those I took in high school, I was craving a new learning experience. I began taking computer science courses my sophomore year of college having no prior expereince in the field. As someone who has always loved learning and excelled academically, I wanted the challenge of trying soemthing new. I was originally drawn to computer science as a tool because it is no secret that technology is playing an increasing role in all job fields. After taking my first few courses and getting past the steep learning curve, I have come to enjoy it more than I ever thought possible.

Professional Goals

I would like to do something that makes a meaningful difference. I want to work with real people to help real people. If I can feel proud of my contributions and see the impact my work makes, I'll know I'm successful.
